Let's talk details.

Who?

All elementary school–aged children are welcome!

  • Grades: K–5
  • Number of children: This fall, we’re limiting our program to 20 participants. Enrollment is first come, first served.

What?

Weekly, 2-hour sessions:

  • Hour 1 – The Voice: Exploring the instrument itself and how we relate to it.
  • Hour 2 – The Music: Singing together and preparing for our performance.

In the final week, families are invited to a special performance, with a professional video provided so you can relax and enjoy the moment.

When?

Sundays, 1–3pm for 9 weeks

  • Start: September 21st
  • End + Performance: November 16th (2–3pm)

Where?

Advent Lutheran Church

  • Address: 2504 Broadway, New York, NY 10025
  • Cross streets: 93rd & Broadway

Tuition & Payment Options

  • Full Price: $900
  • Option 1 - Pay In Full: $800 ($100 discount!)
  • Option 2 - Pay Monthly: 3 payments of $300
